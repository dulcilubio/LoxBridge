# LoxBridge

**LoxBridge** automatically bridges your orienteering runs to [Livelox](https://www.livelox.com) — no cables, no manual exports.

After each workout recorded on Apple Watch or iPhone, LoxBridge extracts the GPS route from Apple Health and uploads it to Livelox, ready to replay and share.

---

## Features

- Automatic route upload after every outdoor workout
- Supports Apple Watch and iPhone GPS recordings
- GPX export and sharing
- Route history with status tracking
- Livelox OAuth integration
- Notifications when routes are ready

## Requirements

- iPhone with iOS 17+
- Apple Watch (optional, recommended)
- A [Livelox](https://www.livelox.com) account

## Privacy

LoxBridge does not operate its own servers. All processing happens on your device. GPS data is only sent to Livelox when you initiate an upload.

Full privacy policy: **https://dulcilubio.github.io/LoxBridge/**
